Neurror

Neurror bridges the gap between mental health and society by making brain activity visible and understandable. It shows how your brain behaves in different states and offers simple ways to rebalance.

Overthinking, for example, is linked to the Default Mode Network (DMN), which keeps thoughts looping. Holding a cold object for a few seconds can interrupt this cycle, shifting brain activity and restoring clarity.

By understanding these patterns, Neurror helps turn awareness into action.
